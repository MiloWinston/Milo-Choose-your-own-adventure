You wander off and away from the park untile you find yourself in a forest. You see a house in the middle of the forest and the door is open, so you deside to go in.

As you walk in the alien spaceship you notice that there is nobody there. Suddenly you feel a paralizing shock on your back. One of the aliens has shot you and the last thing you see before you died is its purple face staring at you! What is your reaction to that?

As you walk into the house you notice that the fireplace still has embers in it. "This must mean that someone has been here recently!" you conclude. Then you look up and see a massive spider the size of a car attached to the cieling. You try to run away, but it is too late, the spider falls on you and kills you. What is your reaction?
